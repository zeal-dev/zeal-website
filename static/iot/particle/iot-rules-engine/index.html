Works seamlessly with the Particle ecosystem

The Rules Engine integrates out of the box with Particle's IoT devices and Device Cloud.

Stand on the shoulders of giants

The Rules Engine is built on top of Node-RED, a widely adopted open source tool. Thousands of community-contributed logic snippets, called nodes, are at your fingertips to supercharge your workflows.

Particle is also a proud member of the JS Foundation to support the Node-RED project and other open source JavaScript initiatives.

Get your IoT data where you need it to go

Microsoft shop? Use Salesforce for CRM? No problem. The Rules Engine supports integrations with any service with an API so you can seamlessly connect device data with the tools your business relies on. It's your data, after all.

Democratize IoT development

Anyone on your team can use the Rules Engine, not just engineers. A drag-and-drop visual programming interface empowers your entire organization to design and manage your IoT cloud architecture.

Enlist your customer success team to create support-related flows when devices experience problems. Call on business analysts to create marketing automation based on sensor data. It's all possible with the IoT Rules Engine.
