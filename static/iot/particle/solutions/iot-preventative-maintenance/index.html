Why preventative maintenance?

Preventative maintenance limits inefficiencies, improves safety, and saves you money.

  • 50%

    Less downtime

    IoT-based preventative maintenance can reduce asset downtime by 50%.

  • 30%

    Cost reductions

    Companies who implement preventative maintenance solutions reduce costs by 30%.

  • 25%

    Fewer injuries

    Fixing machines before they break can increase employee safety by 25%.

Jacuzzi saves millions with IoT-based preventative maintenance

With Particle's platform, Jacuzzi hot tubs are connected to the Internet and can notify owners and local dealers when maintenance needs to occur.

With increased visibility into the state and location of each tub, Jacuzzi plans to save millions thanks to IoT-based preventative maintenance.

Industrial Applications

Connect your machines and equipment to monitor when they need to be fixed.

  • Transform field services

    Collect data to determine the cause of maintenance issues before sending technicians into the field.

  • Enhance workplace safety

    Broken equipment pose severe safety risks. Receive alerts before they break with IoT sensors.

  • Maximize equipment utilization

    Remotely monitor equipment performance in real-time and react to failures instantly.

  • Improve customer satisfaction

    Optimize customer services by diagnosing and fixing maintenance issues remotely the minute they occur.
